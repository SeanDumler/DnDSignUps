I welcome you to join us on a fantastic story of marvelous wonder, nail-biting action, and wholesome fun! I want to introduce you to...Dungeons and Dragons!!!

Now I know what you're thinking...I am just your average joe, with a 9-5 job. How can I be a tall tale hero who saves a city? How can I be a stealthy, world renowned thief aiming to steal the most precious jewels? The answer is simple, IMAGINATION! Here, check out this video below by TheDungeoncast who explains what D&D is, how to play, and why you should start playing!


See? It isn't as overwhelming as you would think! There are thousands, if not millions of players around the world playing Dungeons and Dragons. I myself play in 3 different campaigns with 3 different stories and 3 different characters. Trust me when I say, we all have a blast playing! Here are some reasons why I think you should give it a try:

  • It's a chance to explore your imagination
  • Explore a new fantasy world that you get to decide how you play
  • Make new friends who have the same appreciation and love of adventure and excitement
  • Have fun gaining new gear, leveling up, fighting monsters and bosses, developing a story
  • Dice and minis! Oh and more dice! Did I mention dice?
